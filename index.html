How Does Unemployment Happen?

The answer is sort of complicated. This is because there isn’t exactly one reason for unemployment. Unemployment can be a result of location, the part of town that you live in, the building that you work in, or even the country that you live in.

Of course location isn't the only thing that can affect unemployment. Parts of who you are as a person can affect whether you get a job or not, things like your gender and race can play a part in being employed.

We hope our animation will make a difference to unemployment to you, our audience, by showing you the worst-case-scenario. We know that seeing such a heavy topic presented so lightly can seem a little disheartneing, but we hope that with this you (and everyone else reading), will be more motivated to make a difference where you can, whether its learning and informing others of the cause, or working to end unfairness in the workplace.

Meet The Cast

Mr. Blob

Mr. Blob, our main character proteganoist. All the events in our animation focus on Mr. Blob, Mr. Blob, was fired from his job one day by Big Red, while reasoning is unclear as to exactly why he was fired we know one thing for sure, it wasn't right.

Big Red

Big Red, our antagonist. Big Red is the owner of the company that Mr. Blob works for. Big Red is particularly against people like Mr. Blob, and decided that it would be a benefit to all to eliminate Mr. Blob from the work force.

Mrs. Blob

Mrs. Blob, the (ex) wife of Mr. Blob, Mr. and Mrs. Blob are happily married! Until of course Mr. Blob is fired from his job, Mrs. Blob doesn't like this amd decides to take the kids and leave Mr. Blob.

Tommy

Tommy, The first child of Mr. and Mrs. Blob. Tommy doens't do much but we love him anyway, he's there for the ride and is unfortunately seeing his family fall apart.

Billy

Billy, the second child of Mr. and Mrs. Blob. Like Tommy, Billy is just along for the ride completely oblivious to how all of this mioght affect him in the future.

Location, Location, Location

We cycled through combinations of these three walls and floors to create different rooms for our animation to take place in, such as Big red's office, Mr. Blob's home, and outside.

The Plot

In our story there are (roughly) four main events. Event Number One: Big Red Fired Mr. Blob from the company, this leaves Mr. Blob out of work for the rest of the story. Event Number Two: Mr. Blob tells his wife that he lost his job, Mrs. Blob is unhappy about this, so she takes the kids, picks up and leaves. Event Number Three: Mr. Blob slowly goes into debt and is eventually evicted from his home. After Mr. Blob is evicted from his home he ends up on the streets asking for spare change. Event Number Four: Big Red goes driving along the road that Mr. Blob is currently at, and picks up Mr. Blob now ex wife and two children. Very soon after Mr. Blob is.. lights out.
